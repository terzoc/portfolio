Quality Control Study

Method

We studiedwww.ucvts.org, the school's official website. The website's target audience is students, their families, and teachers. We had subjects Navigate to a teacher's website, open PowerSchool, and find the AIT calendar.

Results

Our subjects were able to complete all the tasks. Subjects easily found a teacher page and the calendar, , though finding PowerSchool took the longest. Subjects took around 10-15 steps to complete all the tasks in a reasonable amount of time.

Discussion

We learned that the website has a useful layout. Different sections of the website are labeled and easy to understand. However, PowerSchool was somewhat difficult to find. The link could be moved to a more accessible location, like the header or the main page.
